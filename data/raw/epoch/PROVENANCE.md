Files in this directory:

- `epoch_all_models.csv` — full database of machine-learning models
  with publication dates, training compute (FLOP), parameters,
  training hardware, and training power draw (W) where available.

Access date: 28 April 2026
Access method: direct CSV download from epoch.ai/data
Preprocessing applied at ingestion: none

Notes:

- Coverage on the `Training power draw (W)` column is partial:
  populated for 481 of 2,076 post-2023 models and for 39 of 137
  flagged frontier models.
- Dataset is updated weekly; the file in this directory is a
  point-in-time snapshot. To refresh, re-download from the URL
  above and replace this file.
